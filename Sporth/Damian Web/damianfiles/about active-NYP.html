About Active-NYP











Active-NYP is a club where nyp students are able to book sports facilities to enjoy playing sports with their friends after a stressful day at school or for them to spend time with their friends to exercise whenever they are free as Active-NYP is opened during weekdays,weekends and even public holidays.

How active-NYP came about is that the student union group found out that NYP students are not having a active lifestyle as there isn't any sports facilities in NYP for them to use.Therefore the student union club proposed to the principle of NYP to be able to construct sports facilities for not only the students but for the staff to use and have a active lifestyle.
