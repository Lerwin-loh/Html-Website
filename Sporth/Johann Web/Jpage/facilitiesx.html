Facilities

Bowling

Bowling is cool.

There are 5 bowling lanes. There is also a snack bar. Shoes are provided. You may sign up for bowling lessons if interested at AMK NYP.

The bowling alley is located at level 2 in the building.

Soccer

Soccer is cool.

There are 2 soccer fields. Soccer balls are provided. You may sign up for soccer lessons if interested at AMK NYP.

The soccer fields are located at the back in the building.

Basketball

Basketball is cool.

There are 3 basketball courts. Basketballs are provided. You may sign up for basketball lessons if interested at AMK NYP.

The basketball courts are located at level 2 in the building.

Swimming

Swimming is cool.

There are 2 swimming pools. There is 4 shower rooms. Lifeguards are always available. You may sign up for swimming lessons at AMK NYP.

The swimming pools are located at the back in the building.

Billiard

Billiard is cool.

There are 4 pool tables. Cue sticks are provided and there is a snack bar. Billiard lessons are available for sign up if interested at AMK NYP.

The pool tables are located at level 3 in the building.

Gym

The gym is cool.

The gym is very big. It has all kinds of gym equipments. A towel is neccesary for entrance. A fitness coach is available for hire at AMK NYP.

The gym is located at level 1 in the building.

Dance Studio

Dancing is cool.

There are 3 dance studios with mirrors, props and air-conditioning.

The dance studios are located at level 2 in the building.

Tennis

Tennis is cool.

There are 4 tennis courts. Tennis rackets and balls are provided. The tennis court already has nets available.

The tennis courts are located at level four in the building.
